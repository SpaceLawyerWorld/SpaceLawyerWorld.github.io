Humankind's activities in outer space hold untold potential, but only if we work together.

Our collective journey into outer space can help develop technology, deliver new science and,

unite people and peoples across the globe in common wonder of a common universe

- what astronauts call global consciousness.

 

Most importantly, turning our collective gaze upward can help us imagine.

Imagine a better world, imagine a common future, and

imagine a way forward.

 

Properly achieved, we can explore space for the knowledge it holds.

Together, we will find ways to unite in our common cause. 

we will find ways to save the Earth's environment,

to reach beyond boundaries.

The Outer Space Treaty has lost none of its immense relevance since 1967.

The rights it establishes for humankind are as important today

as they were at the beginning of the space age.

aims to provide a platform for dialogue between space lawyers across the world.

Space lawyers that want to contribute to progressive development,

of space law and the values it protects:

 

The use and exploration of space, for the benefit of all.
